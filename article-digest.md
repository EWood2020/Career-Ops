> "Most AI failures aren't AI problems. They're foundation problems."

The thesis: AI applied to broken processes doesn't fix them — it amplifies the dysfunction.
Organizations that succeed with AI fix the knowledge architecture, process clarity,
and data integrity first. Then they build agents on top.

This is the lens I apply to every client engagement and every employed role I've held.
